Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

SafeDose ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use the SafeDose mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

SafeDose collects only the minimum information needed to provide the service:

  • Medicine photos — images you take or upload are sent to our AI service to identify the medicine. Photos are not stored after processing.
  • Health data — blood pressure, blood sugar, weight and mood readings you log are stored locally on your device.
  • Medicine cabinet data — medicines you save, reminders, and dose history are stored locally on your device.
  • Payment information — handled entirely by Stripe. We never see or store your card details.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To identify medicines and provide safety information via the Claude AI API
  • To send dose reminders and notifications
  • To generate AI health insights from your health data
  • To process subscription payments via Stripe

3. Data Storage

Most of your data is stored locally on your device using your browser's localStorage. We do not run a database of your personal health information. Medicine photos are processed in real time and immediately discarded — we do not retain images.

4. Third-Party Services

SafeDose uses the following third-party services:

  • Anthropic Claude API — for medicine identification and AI responses. See Anthropic's Privacy Policy.
  • Stripe — for payment processing. See Stripe's Privacy Policy.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with third parties except as described above (AI processing and payment handling). We do not serve ads. We do not share your health data with insurance companies, employers, or any other parties.

6. Children's Privacy

SafeDose is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.

7. Your Rights

Since your data is stored locally on your device, you can delete it at any time by clearing your browser/app data. To cancel your subscription, you can do so at any time through your app store or by contacting us.

8. Security

We take security seriously. API keys are stored on our server and never exposed to users. All communication between the app and our servers uses HTTPS encryption.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by updating the date at the top of this page.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your data, please contact us at: support.safedose@gmail.com
